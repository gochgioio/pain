hardest games in the world part 2

Welcome to our guide on the most challenging video games ever made. From intricate action RPGs to fiendishly difficult platformers, this list showcases games that are not only tough but also incredibly rewarding.

  1. Dark Souls

    Known for its challenging gameplay and intricate world design.

  2. Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

    A demanding action game with intense boss battles.

  3. Elden Ring

    An open-world game from the creators of Dark Souls, offering a significant challenge.

  4. Devil May Cry 5

    A hack-and-slash game with a complex combat system.

  5. Celeste

    A 2D platformer that requires precision and skill.

  6. Blasphemous 2

    A Metroidvania game with tough bosses and a nonlinear approach to storytelling.

  7. Darkest Dungeon II

    A party-based game with psychological aspects and challenging combat.

  8. Remnant II

    A cooperative or solo third-person shooter with tough bosses and rich mechanics.

  9. Returnal

    A game with a focus on reflexes and survival in a hostile environment.

  10. Flappy Bird

    A seemingly simple mobile game that is surprisingly challenging.
